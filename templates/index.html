PROGRAMMING ACHIEVEMENTS

My first use case of Data analytics was on the rugby field while trying to provide my team with a competitive advantage over unique opponents. I spent numerous hours analyzing film and keeping track of opponent statistics which mainly was used for defensive purposes, until I thought of building our entire game plan based on entry level descriptive analytics. I trained my team to be able to adapt to all kinds of playing styles, from aggressive and conservative, to risk taking and opportunistic. When used correctly our team went on to a 36-8 record in two seasons. This outcome (and iron man movies) ignited my curiosity on the impact Data Analytics can have in other Domains.

Well, here we are today, just a little over two years have passed from when I decided to switch careers and teach myself to code, read math books from the library, complete some courses and launch a career in machine learning. It would be an incredible opportunity and a privilege to work at a place where my passion for sports and machine learning can coexist as a daily routine.
